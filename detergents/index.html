About Us

We are a chemical company targeted towards improving your hygiene in your homes, churches, schools and other corporate buildings. Our site seeks to help consumers find the products they need in an easier way.

Stormgenix is a company based in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe. Our company started operating in 2020, around the same time that the Covid-19 pandemic started affecting the world. We became an officially registered company in December, 2021. Our desire to start this company was born from our love for the community and our desire to help lessen the life changing impacts of the Covid-19 pandemic. We intended to do so by offering quality cleaning detergents such as dishwashing liquid, pine gels, toilet dip, floor polish and hand sanitizers.

We wish to have a company that takes the form of a one-stop-shop when it comes to buying all the cleaning materials that one would ever need. We will also strive to make sure that the products we distribute to our customers will be environmentally friendly, and will provide guidelines on how to use them such that they do not cause side effects such as allergies and skin irritations.
