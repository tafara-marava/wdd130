Content

Home page

We are a chemical company targeted towards improving your hygiene in your homes, churches, schools and other corporate buildings. Our site seeks to help consumers find the products they need in an easier way. About Us Stormgenix is a company based in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe. Our company started operating in 2020, around the same time that the Covid-19 pandemic started affecting the world. We became an officially registered company in December, 2021. Our desire to start this company was born from our love for the community and our desire to help lessen the life changing impacts of the Covid-19 pandemic. We intended to do so by offering quality cleaning detergents such as dishwashing liquid, pine gels, toilet dip, floor polish and hand sanitizers. Within the next few years, we hope to achieve our dream of being a nationwide company whose services and products are available to use in organisations such as schools, clinics, hospitals, restaurants, butcheries and even in people’s homes. We wish to have a company that takes the form of a one-stop-shop when it comes to buying all the cleaning materials that one would ever need. We will also strive to make sure that the products we distribute to our customers will be environmentally friendly, and will provide guidelines on how to use them such that they do not cause side effects such as allergies and skin irritations

We want you to keep shopping with us Because we value our customers, we have some great deals prepared for them to ensure that they come to our site and enjoy their experience as they continue shopping with us. As a start to our journey in business, we are offering a 10% discount to all our new customers who will purchase some of our products through our website. We will also be offering a 25% discount for every 5 purchases that a customer will make through our website, and a $20 coupon for every person that refers two friends and helps them make a purchase through the website. Payments Different methods of payment such as VIsa, Mastercard, Venmo, Paypal, Apple Pay and many more will be made available to all our customers so that we can help improve their shopping experience. A convenience fee of 1% will be charged for ALL the products that will be bought through our site. Shipping Shipping costs will vary based on your location and the shipping agents that will be used to get our products to our customers’ addresses.

Feedback In an effort to improve user experience on our websites, we will also periodically conduct surveys to find out from our customers how their experience has been with our website. We will begin from their experiences in shipping time to payment methods, the pricing of our products, whether or not they are environmentally friendly and also rate our products on the extent to which they cause side effects and allergic reactions. The feedback that we receive from our customers will be extremely valuable to us, so to compensate them for taking their time to complete our surveys, a few people will be awarded discount coupons for some of the products that we will have on offer. Other Services Stormgenix enterprise will also be offering cleaning services to hospitals, clinics, schools and other professional buildings. The cleaning services will be customised according to our clients’ needs. We will also be offering fumigation services in addition to the chemicals that we will make available to achieve the same purpose. In addition to all these awesome features and deals, we will make sure we strive to generate a fair return on investments to our shareholders and at the same time improve the living standards in local communities and improve the packages that we give to our employees.
